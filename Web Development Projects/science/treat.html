Modern Treatment

Tuberculosis treatment can be a long process. Patients go through a regimen of antibiotics which lasts months, fighting both the bacteria and its side effects, of which some are nausea and liver damage. Drug resistent TB is also a hurdle. Cutting-edge treatments hold promise, but the journey remains uphill.
