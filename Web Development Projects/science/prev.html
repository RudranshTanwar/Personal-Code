Drug Resistant TB

Drug reistant TB is a whole nother beast, which occurs when TB becomes resistant to at least of the most effective TB medicines. Abreviated as DR-TB, it is transmitted in the same way as TB.It has a chance to develop when people with TB quit taking meds before the disease is fully treated, or when outdated drug regimens are used improperly.
